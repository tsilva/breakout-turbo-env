# Code of conduct

We want participation in breakout-turbo-env to be safe, constructive, and
welcoming. Be respectful, assume good intent, give actionable technical
feedback, and make room for people with different experience levels and
backgrounds.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, deliberate
intimidation, doxxing, and sustained disruption are not acceptable in project
spaces. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when behavior harms the community.

Report abuse through GitHub's reporting tools. For a project-specific concern,
contact the maintainer through the contact method on their GitHub profile.
Reports will be handled as confidentially and promptly as practical. Good-faith
reports will not result in retaliation.

This code applies to repository activity, project discussions, and public
events where someone is representing the project.
